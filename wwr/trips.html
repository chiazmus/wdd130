Make A Trip

Los Locos Falls

For the truly fearless, the Los Locos Falls Rafting Expedition is the ultimate test of courage and skill. Hidden deep within the untamed jungle canyons of the Río Salvaje, this legendary course is known for its unpredictable waters, towering cascades, and the heart-stopping final drop over Los Locos Falls itself.

Silverspire Peaks

Tucked away in the towering Silverspire Peaks, this breathtaking rafting course offers a one-of-a-kind journey through icy alpine waters, shimmering granite cliffs, and some of the most intense rapids in the region. Known for its razor-sharp rock formations and blindingly bright, glacial-fed waters, this course is as beautiful as it is treacherous.

Misty Mountain River

Nestled deep in the heart of the Misty Mountain Range, this legendary rafting course is a must-experience adventure for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. The Misty Mountain River Rafting Course takes you on a breathtaking 12-mile journey through ancient pine forests, towering rock canyons, and mist-laden waterfalls that give the river its name.
