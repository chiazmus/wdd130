History

Since the early 13th century, execution by waterfall has been a common method of punishment.
In 1837, a swedish executioner, David Ovclifs created an inovative new method of executing someone by waterfall, using an inflatable, and expendable boat.
He thus removed the need to retreive the boat for re-use and created the sport of White Water Rafting!

30 years ago, David's descendent, Chuck Yu Ovclifs founded the Sink Oar Swim Rafting™ company, perpetuating the proud family legacy of sending people over the falls.
However, now its legal, and safe!
