Once upon a time there was a bard named Sir Bardulus. Sir Bardulus was the best bard in the world,
or so he thought. However, one day the beloved lute of Sir Bardulus was surreptitiously stolen. Sir Bardulus was duely distraught,
and knew not what to do. He searched and searched but to no avail. His beloved lute was lost. Finally, Sir Bardulus sought the
strange fellow named the Searcher of Sarcath. The strange Searcher of Sarcath searched with striking sedulousness, finally finding
in the forlorn forest of fargool the lost lute. It had been tragically taken by the bold bandits who had placed their bastion therin.
Rejoicing at the reunion of his recently retrieved beloved lute, Sir Bardulus the Bard vowed never again to lose his lute!
What does this have to do with me? Absolutely nothing. I hope you enjoyed the story though.
